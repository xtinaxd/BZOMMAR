The Heritage Gardens

Nature as Architecture

The Bzommar Palace Gardens are a living piece of Levantine history. Spanning over 1,200 square meters of manicured flora and wild pine groves, this is a sanctuary designed for moments of absolute scale. Here, the mountain air remains crisp even in the height of summer, and every stone-paved path leads to a view that has remained unchanged for centuries.
